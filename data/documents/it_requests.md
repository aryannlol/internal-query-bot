# IT Requests Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the process for requesting IT hardware, software, access, or support services.

## Request Categories

- New hardware (laptop, monitor, peripherals)
- Software/license requests
- System access or permission changes
- Account setup/modification
- Technical support/incident reporting
- Mobile device management (MDM) enrollment

## Submission Process

- All requests must be submitted through the IT Service Portal (self-service ticketing system).
- Required information: business justification, urgency, cost centre/department code.
- Attachments (quotes, specifications) must be included when applicable.

## Approval Authority

- **Standard requests (under $1,000)**: IT Service Desk Lead
- **Hardware/software over $1,000**: IT Manager + Finance approval
- **Access/permission changes**: System Owner + IT Security
- **Emergency requests outside business hours**: On-call IT Engineer (escalation required)

## Response and Fulfillment SLAs

- **Critical/urgent (business blocking)**: 4 business hours response, 1 business day resolution target
- **High priority**: 8 business hours response, 3 business days resolution target

## Prohibited Actions
Employees may not:

- Purchase IT equipment personally for reimbursement
- Install unapproved software
- Bypass approval processes

All IT requests must be tracked in the service portal. Unauthorized procurement or installation may result in disciplinary action.
