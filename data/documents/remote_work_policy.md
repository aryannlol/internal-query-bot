# Remote Work Policy

## Purpose
This policy defines the conditions under which employees may work remotely.

## Eligibility
Remote work is available to full-time employees whose roles can be performed effectively outside the office, subject to manager approval.  
Contractors and temporary employees are not eligible.

## Approval Authority
- Remote work arrangements must be approved by the employee's direct manager.
- Arrangements exceeding 3 days per week require additional approval from Department Director.

## Work Hours Expectations
- Employees must maintain core business hours (10:00â€“16:00 local time) for availability.
- Full weekly hours as defined in employment agreement must be completed.
- Employees remain responsible for meeting all performance expectations.

## Equipment Responsibility
- Company-provided equipment remains company property.
- Employees are responsible for:
  - Secure storage of equipment
  - Prompt reporting of damage/loss
  - Return of equipment upon termination or role change

Personal equipment may not be used for company work without explicit IT approval.

## Security Requirements for Remote Access
- All remote access must use company-provided VPN.
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all systems.
- Devices must maintain up-to-date antivirus software and OS patches.
- Employees must work from secure, private locations.
- Public Wi-Fi may not be used for company business without approved VPN.

Violations of this policy may result in revocation of remote work privileges and/or disciplinary action.
