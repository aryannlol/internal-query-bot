# Expense Policy

## Purpose
This policy governs the reimbursement of **business-related expenses** incurred by employees while performing their job duties.

## Approval Authority

| Expense Amount          | Required Approver(s)                          | Notes                                      |
|-------------------------|-----------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| Any amount              | Direct Manager                                | Mandatory for all expense reports          |
| > $1,500                | Direct Manager + Department Director          | Additional layer of review                 |
| > $5,000                | Direct Manager + Department Director + Finance Director | Final approval required                  |

## Allowed Expense Categories
The following categories are generally reimbursable when they are **directly related to business purposes**:

- **Travel**  
  Airfare, lodging, ground transportation (taxis, rideshare, rental cars)
- **Meals and Entertainment**  
  Must have a clear **business purpose** and include attendees and discussion topics
- **Office Supplies and Materials**  
  Items necessary for work (approved by manager)
- **Professional Development**  
  Courses, certifications, books, conferences, workshops
- **Client-related Expenses**  
  Gifts, meals, or events directly supporting client relationships
- **Mileage**  
  Business use of personal vehicle (at current IRS standard mileage rate)

## Prohibited Expenses
The following are **not** reimbursable under any circumstances:

- Personal expenses of any kind
- Traffic fines, parking tickets, or other penalties
- Entertainment or meals without a documented business purpose
- Luxury items or upgrades (e.g., first-class travel, premium lodging)
- Alcohol (unless part of a client meal with clear business purpose and pre-approved)
- Expenses already paid/reimbursed by another source

## Monthly Expense Limits

- **Individual monthly reimbursement limit**: **$2,000**  
  Any amount above this requires **pre-approval** from Department Director and Finance
- **Department monthly aggregate limit**: Managed and monitored by the **Department Director**

## Timeline for Approvals and Payouts

- **Manager approval**: Within **5 business days** of submission
- **Finance review & processing**: Within **7 business days** of final approval
- **Payment**: Issued via **direct deposit** on the **next scheduled payroll cycle** (typically bi-weekly)

**Note**: Delays in submission or missing documentation may result in delayed reimbursement.

## Compliance
All employees are responsible for understanding and adhering to this policy.  
**Violations** may result in:

- Denial of reimbursement
- Repayment of improperly reimbursed amounts
- Disciplinary action, up to and including termination
