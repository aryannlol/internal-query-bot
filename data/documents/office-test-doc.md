# Internal Office Policy: TechFlow Solutions

## 1. Remote Work Policy
Employees are eligible for "Focus Fridays," which allows for 100% remote work on the last Friday of every month. For all other days, a hybrid model is followed requiring at least 3 days in the downtown office.

## 2. Equipment Reimbursement
The company provides a one-time "Home Office Stipend" of $850. This can be used for ergonomic chairs, standing desks, or monitor mounts. Receipts must be submitted via the 'ExpenseIt' portal within 30 days of purchase.

## 3. Leave and Time Off
- **Parenthood Leave:** 16 weeks of fully paid leave.
- **Volunteer Days:** 2 days per year for community service.
- **Pet Bereavement:** 1 day of paid leave for the loss of a domestic pet.

## 4. Security Protocols
All employees must rotate their internal database passwords every 90 days. The password must be at least 14 characters long and include at least one special character from the set: ! @ # $ %.

## 5. Contact Information
For emergency IT support after hours, call the "Blue Line" at 555-0199. For payroll queries, email `finance-ops@techflow.internal`.
