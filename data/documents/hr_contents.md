# HR Contacts Directory

## Purpose
This document provides the **current primary points of contact** within the Human Resources department for various employee needs.

## Core HR Team

| Role                              | Responsibilities                                                                 | Primary Contact                          | Best Way to Reach                          |
|-----------------------------------|----------------------------------------------------------------------------------|------------------------------------------|--------------------------------------------|
| **HR Director**                   | Overall HR strategy, policy exceptions, employee relations escalations          | hr.director@company.com                  | Email (non-urgent) or mobile (emergencies) |
| **HR Business Partner**           | Performance management, development plans, leave escalations (assigned by department) | hr.bp@company.com                        | Email – **include your department** in subject line |
| **Payroll & Benefits Specialist** | Salary, bonuses, benefits enrollment, deductions, payroll questions             | payroll@company.com                      | Email                                      |
| **Recruitment Coordinator**       | Job postings, candidate coordination, interview scheduling, onboarding          | recruitment@company.com                  | Email                                      |
| **People Operations Administrator** | Leave requests, general policy questions, documentation, records management   | peopleops@company.com                    | Email or HR Service Portal                 |

## Emergency & After-Hours Contacts

- **Serious employee welfare issues** (e.g., safety, mental health crisis, harassment):  
  Contact **HR Director** immediately via **mobile number** (available in the company emergency contact list)

- **Immediate payroll errors** affecting current pay period:  
  Contact **Payroll Specialist** through the **on-call rotation** (details in emergency contact list)

## How to Contact HR – Quick Guide

| Type of Inquiry                   | Recommended Method                              | Notes                                                                 |
|-----------------------------------|-------------------------------------------------|-----------------------------------------------------------------------|
| **Routine / General questions**   | HR Service Portal (preferred) or email          | Fastest response; tracked for follow-up                               |
| **Confidential / Sensitive**      | Direct email to HR Business Partner or HR Director | Use secure channels; mark subject as “Confidential” if appropriate    |
| **Urgent (same-day response needed)** | Phone call + follow-up email                   | Call first, then send email summarizing the issue for the record      |
| **Leave requests, forms, documentation** | People Operations email or HR Service Portal | Include all relevant details and attachments                          |

## Important Notes
- Contacts are **reviewed and updated quarterly** or whenever there is a personnel change.
- For the **most current information**, always refer to the **internal HR SharePoint page** or the company directory.
- When emailing, please use a **clear subject line** and include your **department**, **employee ID** (if applicable), and any relevant context to ensure the fastest possible response.
